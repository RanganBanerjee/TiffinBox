Our Story

Bringing the taste of home to your workplace

Tiffin Box was born from a simple idea: corporate employees and students away from home deserve to enjoy delicious, homemade meals just like they would at home. We partner with talented home chefs ("aunties") who prepare authentic Bengali dishes with love and care.

Our focus is on Salt Lake and Lake Town areas, serving busy professionals and students who crave the comfort of homemade food. We change our menu daily to ensure variety and freshness, just like your mother would.
